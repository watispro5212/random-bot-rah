Service Directives.

Guidelines for interaction with the Nexus neural framework.

01 // Uplink Authorization

By establishing an uplink, you authorize Nexus to manage roles, messages, and administrative data within your sector.

Users are prohibited from attempting to breach the Singularity Core or manipulate neural economy values via exploits.

03 // Termination

Founders reserve the right to revoke sectoral access at any time for protocol violations.
